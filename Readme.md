Virtual Enumeration of Locations for Optimal City & Interstitial Teleportation Yields

V.E.L.O.C.I.T.Y. is a high-performance waypoint search module for Blish HUD. It allows you to quickly find any waypoint in Guild Wars 2 and copy the chat link to your clipboard instantly, bypassing the need to open the world map and hunt for icons.

Smart Scrolling: Supports viewing up to 100 results at once with a dedicated scrollbar.

Hotkey Support: Open the search bar with a customizable key combination (Default: Ctrl + Shift + F).

Press Ctrl + Shift + F to open the search bar.

Type at least 2 characters (e.g., "sh" or "way").

Drag the top title bar to reposition the window anywhere on your screen.
